Code of Conduct/Team Contract


1. As a member, I will attend all meetings with the group.
   1. If I cannot attend, I will send a notification as soon as possible to the other members to let them know.
2. During the meetings, I will be actively participating.
3. I will make an effort to understand other members’ work.
   1. If I do not understand something, I will ask them for clarification.
4. If someone asks any questions about my work, it is my responsibility to answer those questions.
5. For communication, I will use the Slack channel group-6.
